Introduction to Ci

The Continuous Index Protocol was created with the goal of protect cryptocurrency investment providing a secure dynamic DEFI model.

High price volatility, the outcome of a battle between bullish and bearish speculators, has caused concern among most cryptocurrency owners, who fear losing their investment in hours or days.

There have always been successful investment possibilities in the past, and there will always be profitable investment opportunities in the future; the largest disadvantage has always been risk. Many investors are unable to withstand this pressure and either abandon their investments or lose money.

Today, new technologies have brought with them the revolutionary idea of managing economic assets digitally and in a decentralized way. They have given investors new and ingenious ways to make their money profitable. But in the centralized financial world there has always been a huge drawback: the problem of price manipulation.

Fortunately or unfortunately, until now, the novel decentralized finance or DEFI did not include prices in this decentralization to safeguard them from centralized manipulation by governments, banks or influential people or robotic AI (whales).

The changing value of cryptoassets is expressed in currencies of the centralized or real world where tangible and necessary things like food and houses are bought. Therefore, manipulators in the centralized world can also manipulate prices in the decentralized world.

The manipulation of the markets, their prices and volumes can be accidental or intentional and occurs mainly in two ways:

> Through a large investment (buying or selling) of an asset by large portfolios (whales), coming from a robotic algorithm, a human consciousness or an artificial intelligence (AI) consciousness.
> By issuing news or communiqués (coming from influential people, governmental organizations or random events).

Both drastically volatilize the price leaving many investors, who saw their savings vanish, encouraged to invest them by a promise of the ideal of making quick money.

A chance for quite and freedom investments

The Ci project was born with the aim of providing a solution to this dramatic handicap that seems to haunt the investment world like a curse. Unfortunately, also the cryptographic world, which from the beginning claims to be the solution to the problem of centralization, where "one" could control everything.

Bitcoin, to a significant extent, addressed critical issues such as decentralization of non-manipulable data and anonymity, but it also perpetuated the "curse" of price manipulation through large capital investments or informational sway by popular news or commentary. A simple "tweet" from a well-known person has been enough to send prices, and even entire crypto projects, into a multi-year bear market.

This is not beneficial, it is not desirable, and it should not occur. Where we are advocates of blockchain and the concept of decentralization with all the amazing things it has brought to humanity, we have been thinking how to bring a pragmatic solution no matter how much resistance may come at first.

Although a part of the community is happy with this manipulation issue, because they are obviously profiting from it (as it has already happened and happens in traditional investments), Ci is born to offer one more alternative, as we think that in the innovative crypto world it is necessary to continue evolving the concept of decentralization and financial freedom.

Some more "aggressive" investors may think that it is a boring and predictable project because they know at all times the trajectory that a price will take. Ci was not built for this type of profile, nor for "whales" (human, robotic or AI) that have fun changing prices at will. On the contrary, Ci was born to protect the crypto investment world from them.

What is Ci?

The Continuous index protocol (or CiP from now on) can be implemented as an ERC-20 extended Solidity smart contract (based on OpenZeppelin ERC-20 open source) on the Ethereum blockchain and other compatible ethereum virtual machine (EVM) networks.

CiP make possible an stable and continuously bullish decentralized calculation based on the simple interest formula to regulate it over time.


A = P ( 1 + r * t )

Where:

A = Total accrued amount (principal + interest)
P = Principal investment
r = Rate of Interest per second in decimal
t = Time period involved in seconds passed



Warns that Solidity cannot calculate with decimals, therefore the values must be converted to integer previously and set in the smart contract code. This value is fixed and cannot be modified.

The price of a coin managed by the Ci Protocol ERC-20 extended smart contract, appreciates with each passing second at an interest rate fixed and predetermined in the smart contract.


The formula for calculating the coin price is simple and can be summarized as follows:


i * r * t

Where:

i = initial investment
r = rent per second
t = elapsed seconds


The resulting value is based on the immutable and universal variable time, so any person or machine could calculate the price of this crypto-index at any time, being the result of its calculation unmanipulable and unalterable.

Neither influential investors nor any kind of event can affect the continuity of the price. This makes it the safest cryptographic security in terms of profitability.

These rules are unbreakable and are proctored by the publicly-source smart contract.

From anywhere in the world and any Ethereum wallet that connects to that smart contract through its protocol, is possible to trade this hypothetical cryptoasset without intermediaries, without government controls and without the concern of malicious investors negatively affecting the price.


Why not a compound interest?

If the protocol were to use compound interest then in a few decades it would create an unsustainable bubble and inevitable bankruptcy. In reality this is what happens every so often with common stocks and other crypto assets. There are "pump and dumps" constantly, some lose so that others gain.
By now you will have realized that this is what our project is trying to avoid :)

With the model based on simple interest, there is a linear increase so smooth that to reach the best market cap that Bitcoin has been in 2021 would take over 400 years in the CiP DEFI model. It is also known that in 2021 a small percentage of investors acquire Bitcoin (maybe 15%) and in the next few years a real mass explosion in crypto finance is expected. This will greatly increase Ethereum market capital available.

Therefore, the DEFI model proposed for CiP is dynamically sustained for centuries without the possibility of a "dump" because it is self-regulating by minting and burning token over time.
It is a perfect model in today's unforgiving financial world. Especially considering that the longer the time with token in possession, the higher the sale value.

Fundamentals of hypothetical token

A token based on CiP ERC-20 extended contract is founded with features that make it special and different from any other cryptoasset available in the crypto universe.

The Ci protocol smart contract is part of the ERC-20 extended smart contract and they are complementary. It controls autonomously and without any type of external human authority that what is established by the developer continuously is complied with.


Self-Regulation

The minting and burning of the token is regulated by supply and demand, but not in the way most cryptoassets operate.

The default amount supplied is 0 (zero).

It is minted or burned depending on supply and demand from owners, but under special rules that make it different.

It is not minted as long as only one owner has the token for sale, therefore, the amount of total supply will not increase as long as there is only one owner with tokens for sale. On the other hand, the supply is burned when owners puts the token up for sale and it is exchanged for part of the deposit.

Therefore, the token is constantly and highly deflationary.
This is the key to maintaining it self-regulating and valuable over time despite its constant revaluation

However, because the concept is new, the creator of the contract could reserves the right to limit the max supply or stop minting to increase the intrinsic value of the project (not of the coin that constantly revalues over time). This in turn would benefit its owners and making it in a scarce security that many wish to own.





The Continuity and Stability

The suggested initial value of the a CiP token, like any other stable coin, started with an equivalence of 1 ETH (or network native coin) as the reference currency in the smart contract.

This initial value is established on a specific date expressed in  UNIX Timestamp format.

From that moment the price of the asset is revaluing at x% simple interest rate every second that passes, with no reversals and forever as it is established by the Ci protocol.

To calculate the current price we only need the simple interest formula and two data, the start date and the end date (current date) in UNIX Timestamp format.

Solidity:
function CurrentCoinPrice() public view returns (uint){
                        uint secondsPassed = block.timestamp - InitTimeStamp;        
                        uint secondRate = x; //Unknow for this example
                        return 1000000000000000000 + secondsPassed * secondRate;
                    }


The curent price of the asset can be called to the smart contract from a DWeb:

Web3.js:

                 /*The price will be returned in a native wei uint*/
              })



Anonymity

The frontend that manages the contract does not need to create accounts, collect personal data, or store any data in centralized databases. The only data that smart contract manages is its anonymous wallet and balances.


Transferability

A CiP token can be transferred at any time to any Ethereum network compatible wallet where you trade, without any commission from the smart contract.


Commissions

There are no commissions for buying or transferring CiP token. The commission per sale will occur only once the tokens are sold and you have been paid in ETH (or native currency of the network in which you operate) the value equivalent to the current price for the amount of token sold. The standard commission per sale is established by the smart contract owner and can be called.


Security

CiP's token constant revaluation of value per contract, make it the most secure as well as profitable cryptocurrency or cryptoasset in known history. In addition, it is based on a public-code smart contract that the Ethereum network (or others compatible with its EVM, recommending the ZK (Zero Knowledge) Rollups to increase security and speed) manages in a decentralized way, so you with your wallet private key have full control of the management of your tokens.


Governance

The value of token is marked by the UNIX Time-stamp  of GMT (Greenwich Mean Time) or Coordinated Universal Time or UTC+0.

Neither the smart contract, nor the creator of the contract, nor the owners, nor the agents have any power or control over its price. Since all the information of all the operations of the smart contract that manages it and its code are public, we could then state that there is no decision that is going to affect its price.

Smart contract is audited and could be publicly deployed in a EVM compatible blockchains. In reality the Ci protocol is finished and does not need to make transcendental changes that would negatively affect its owners, however if any decision were to be made that would affect the token and/or its owners or agents, it would be consulted and decision would be made based on anonymous voting of all its owners.

Perpetuality and independence of the project

All the code is public, anyone can read it to check that everything will run reliably.
The rules established for the management of the token price are complied with by the smart contract without exception.

The protocol communicates the smart contract with a frontend (web3.js in our case) that is hosted in a decentralized way on IPFS or Skynet (Sia) servers. (any Ex, Dex, Web, DWeb, App or DApp could integrate it without permissions. They could never have any control over the project)

All this offers complete security, reliability, perpetuality and independence if the project runs on a secure, decentralized and independent network like Ethereum.

About the founder

The Ci Protocol project is the brainchild of developer Hermes Ateneo, who developed it to provide a practical solution to a financial dualism existing in the crypto world, where either the investment is exposed to high volatility, or the investment is stuck in a stable cryptocurrency.

Probing into the concerns and desires of most investors he found that security and peace of mind were, behind profitability, the second most sought after but least obtained factor.

Bitcoin and Ethereum's blockchains provided security and decentralization, which others replicated for profit. Neither, on the other hand, provided a breakthrough on the subject of values manipulation.

Hermes believed that investing in cryptoassets may provide even more freedom if prices could be secured against the manipulation that "powerful" people can always engage in.
This gave Hermes the concept for CiP, which he developed and made available to the general public.
